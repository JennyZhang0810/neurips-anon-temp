🧩 Method Overview

S²-Det consists of a Structure-Aware Prior (semantic, scale, density), Supervised Identity Injection (clean RGBA-based diffusion compositing), and explicit Label Inheritance to avoid detection noise.

📋 Detector-Facing Contract

  • ✅ Labels remain valid after rendering — visible, class-consistent, aligned boxes
  • ✅ Objects appear in semantically compatible contexts — e.g., ships only on water
  • ✅ Scale and density match target distribution — prevents degenerate priors
  • ✅ Artifacts must not introduce shortcuts — frequency audit before training
